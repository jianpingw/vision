Our Story

Jerusalem Tech Blog was established in 2025 by a group of developers, entrepreneurs, and tech enthusiasts who recognized the need for a dedicated platform to showcase Jerusalem's growing tech ecosystem. What began as a small passion project has evolved into a comprehensive resource for the local tech community and a window into Jerusalem's innovation landscape for the wider world.

We're proud to be part of Jerusalem's tech journey and excited to document its continued growth and evolution. As the city's tech scene expands, so too will our coverage and community.
